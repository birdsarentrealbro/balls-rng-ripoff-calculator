Ball Rarity Calculator

Select a base rarity, then add one or more variant traits to calculate the combined probability. The formula used is: Combined Probability = P(Rarity) × P(VariantTrait1) × P(VariantTrait2) × ... where P(Rarity) = Rarity.Weight / TotalRarityWeight, and P(VariantTrait) = Variant.Weight / TotalVariantWeight.

Note: The "Normal" variant cannot be combined with other variants. Selecting "Normal" will disable other variants, and selecting any other variant will disable "Normal".
